Frequently Asked Questions

What happens to unfinished tasks at the end of the day?
When you open the app on a new day, you'll be prompted to roll over any uncompleted tasks. You can keep them on your list, send them back to the backlog, or dismiss them. Each rollover is tracked so you can spot tasks you keep avoiding.
How do I restore my Premium purchase on a new device?
Go to Settings → Premium → Restore purchase. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. Premium is a one-time purchase tied to your Apple ID — no subscription needed.
Is my data backed up?
All data is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud Drive enabled for Just Do Three in your device Settings, your tasks and history will sync automatically across your devices via iCloud.
How do I set up morning and evening reminders?
Go to Settings → Reminders. Toggle on Morning reminder or Evening check-in, then tap the time to choose when you'd like to be reminded. Notifications are scheduled locally — no account needed.
What's included in Premium?
Premium is a one-time purchase that unlocks: 7-day advance planning, day/week/month/year analytics, weekly and monthly recurring tasks, stretch goal tracking, bulk task import from .txt or .csv files, and the most-avoided tasks report.
How do I delete a task permanently?
In the Backlog tab, tap the trash icon next to any task and confirm deletion. Tasks can also be removed from today's list using the ··· menu on each task card — this returns them to the backlog rather than deleting them.
Can I import a list of tasks all at once?
Yes — with Premium. Tap the import icon in the Backlog tab (or use the Add Task sheet) to upload a .txt or .csv file. Each line becomes a task. For CSV files, only the first column is used.
Does Just Do Three collect any of my data?
No. The app collects no personal information, analytics, or usage data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
